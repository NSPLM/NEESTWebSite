Spindle Bearing Protection

Spindle crashes cost upwards of $10,000. Neest detects micro-vibrations and heat anomalies, texting your operator to pause the job hours before the bearing seizes.

Coolant & Temp Alerts

Stop scrap before the mold opens.

In blow molding, a 5-degree temperature shift means thousands of defective parts. Neest monitors your extruders, chillers, and hydraulics to catch process drift before it hits your scrap rate.

🔥

Extruder Temp Anomalies

Resin too hot? Parison drooping? The machine will text your operator instantly if barrel heater bands start failing, saving you from a massive material purge.

❄️

Chiller & Cycle Time Alerts

Mold cooling is where you make money. If chiller flow drops and your cycle times start creeping up, Neest alerts maintenance to check the lines before production drops.

💧

Hydraulic Pressure Drops

A slight drop in clamp pressure can cause blown-out seams. We track the hydraulic lines and translate pressure faults into a simple text: "Clamp pressure low, check pump #2."
